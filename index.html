NEFERTITI

The Life and Legacy of an Ancient Egyptian Queen

Who is Nefertiti?

Nefertiti, one of ancient Egypt's most enigmatic and influential figures, was queen alongside Pharaoh Akhenaten during a period of major cultural transformation (1353–1336 BCE). She played a key role in the dramatic shift toward the worship of the sun god Aten, a move that redefined Egypt’s religious and political landscape. Nefertiti is renowned for her iconic painted sandstone bust, discovered in 1913, which remains a global symbol of beauty and power.
